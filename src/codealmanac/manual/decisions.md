# Decisions

A decision page records one architecturally meaningful choice.

The lead should summarize the choice, the reason it exists, and the consequence
future work must respect.

Use the useful parts of ADRs:

- Status
- Context
- Decision
- Consequences

The context section states the forces and tradeoffs. The decision section
states the chosen path. The consequences section explains what becomes easier,
harder, required, or forbidden because of the choice.

Do not bundle several unrelated choices into one decision page.
